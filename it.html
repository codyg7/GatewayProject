Our IT Scene!

Google
Google Careers

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

Accenture
Accenture Careers

Accenture has three locations across the Dublin Docklands. Delving into AI, predictive analytics, blockchain and mixed reality, Accenture’s Irish incubation hub—known as The Dock—brings new ideas to life for Fortune 500 companies in nearly 50 different industries.

Twitter
Twitter Careers

Twitter is an American microblogging and social networking service on which users post and interact with messages known as "tweets". Registered users can post, like, and retweet tweets, but unregistered users can only read them.

LinkedIn
LinkedIn Careers

LinkedIn is an American business and employment-oriented service that operates via websites and mobile apps. it is mainly used for professional networking, including employers posting jobs and job seekers posting their CVs.

MongoDB
MongoDB Careers

MongoDB is a software company that develops and provides commercial support for the leading modern, general purpose database MongoDB, a non-relational database that stores data in JSON-like documents with flexible schemas.

Amazon Web Services
Amazon Web Servies Careers

Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides millions of companies with secure, on-demand cloud computing services, including database, storage and content delivery. It powers high-profile companies like Netflix and NASA, as well as hundreds of thousands of small businesses and startups in 190 countries

Indeed
Indeed Careers

LIndeed is an American worldwide employment-related search engine for job listings.

Udemy
Udemy Careers

Udemy is an online learning platform aimed at professional adults and students. The platform has more than 50 million students and 57,000 instructors teaching courses in over 65 languages. There have been over 295 million course enrollments.
